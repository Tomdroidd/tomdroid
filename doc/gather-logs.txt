http://developer.android.com/sdk/installing/bundle.html

Once everything is installed. If you are a CLI type of person, plug in your android device and do:

  $ adb logcat

Otherwise you can get the logcat out of DDMS (D<something> Device Manager...) accessible from the Eclipse IDE.

On Windows you might need a USB driver from google or your phone vendor: http://developer.android.com/sdk/win-usb.html

On OS X or Linux you might need a udev entry: http://developer.android.com/tools/device.html

logcat - http://developer.android.com/tools/help/logcat.html

Might seem complicated but it's not that bad actually. Do not hesitate to ask for further indications. Cheers!
